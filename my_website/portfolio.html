Interactive Fiction

Summary

The story is about a student from United State who start a new life in Japan. How the student will live will depend on the choice player make. There are only one happy ending and the rest is doom. Therefore, choose wisely.

Reflection

a. To make this story, I have to learn the basic of python or at least know about the basic of coding.

b. Since this is a basic program, I did not have many big problem with it. But the problem is that I have to watch out for where I put in the choice so the story can move on. There are time I put the choice in the wrong part of the story and I have to look through the code to see where the choice go.

c.I have a lot of fun with the story and program, but the part I dislike is the writing since I have to watch out for grammar and spelling errors.

d.I particularly like "The Great Escape" and "Hi Miss Nguyen", these story have many choices and result in simple ending.

e. If I had a chance to do my story again then I would like to fix the grammars and find a more clever way to write the story better.
